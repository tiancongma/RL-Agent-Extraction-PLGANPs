- When a concrete value is explicitly selected or chosen as optimal and later explicitly reused, emit that literal value.

FORBIDDEN BEHAVIORS
- Do not enumerate full formulation rows.
- Do not perform final structured row construction.
- Do not perform deterministic expansion, row materialization, normalization, arbitration, or inheritance closure.
- Do not assume missing evidence or invent unsampled rows.
