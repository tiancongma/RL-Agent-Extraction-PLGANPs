## Abstract
The purpose of this research was to develop a new kind of GA-CS/sPLGA complexed
Microspheres delivery system for CuB. In this work, star-shaped poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid)
(sPLGA-COOH) and chitosan modified with glycyrrhetic acid (GA-CS) were successfully
synthesized. The GA-CS/sPLGA complexed Microspheres for CuB was prepared by emulsion
evaporation method. Futhermore, CuB-loaded Microspheres formulations were optimized. The
release behaviour of CuB-loaded Microspheres in vitro were also examined. It was showed that
CuB-loaded amount of Microspheres could reach 58.78%+-1.288 by adding GA-CS into the
external water phase. The size of corresponding CuB-loaded Microspheres was 370+-16.14 nm in
diameter, and zeta potential was negative 10.86+-0.4790 mV measured by laser light particle
size/zeta potential analysis. The results indicated that the complexed GA-CS/sPLGA Microspheres

2.5 Preparation
of
Drug-loaded
Complexed sPLGA Microspheress

Briefly, 160 mg sPLGA-COOH and 40 mg CuB
were dissolved in 2 mL dichloromethane as the
oil phase, and an apropriate amount of GA-CS
(or CS) was dissolved in 20 mL 2% PVA solution
as the water phase. Under ultrasound action the

oil phase was slowly dropped into water phase to
form a stable emulsified solution in an ice bath
(0degC). The obtained solution was then poured
into a large amount of 2% PVA solution, and the
mixed solution was continually stirred for 6 h to
volatilize dichloromethane completely. Pure CuBloaded
Microspheres
were
obtained
by
centrifugation at 3,500 rpm for 30 min, followed
by washing with 2% aqueous solution of ethanol
(3x10 mL) and vacuum drying for 48 hours.

Drug entrapment efficiency, drug-loaded amount,
particle size and zeta patential were chosen as
indexes, and the preparation formulation of drugloaded Microspheres was optimized by changing
the amount and types of chitosan added in the
external water phase.

Entrapment Efficiency of CuB

5 mg CuB-loaded Microspheres were put into a
10 mL volumetric flask. Then 2 mL of
dichloromethane was added to dissolve the
Microspheres
completely
under

action. Finally, 8 mL of methanol was added and
the solution was filtered through 0.22 um

membrane.
After
abandoning
primary filtrate, subsequent filtrate was collected.
CuB concentration in the filtrate was determined
by HPLC under the following chromatographic
conditions
[Chromatographic
column:
Shimpack VP-ODS C18 (200 mmx4.6 mm, 5 um),
mobile phase of methanol: water (70:30), flow
rate: 1 mL*min-1, column temperature: 30degC,
detection wavelength: 228 nm, injection volume:
20 uL]. Based on the standard curve of CuB and
the following formulas, the drug-loaded amount
and entrapment efficiency were calculated.

The amount of drug-loaded Microspheress = The
drug weight contained in the Microspheres/The
total weight of Microspheresx100%;

The entrapment efficiency = The drug weight
contained in the Microspheres/The total weight of
the drugx100%.

ligand
GA
was
introduced
to
the
Microspheres by adding GA-CS in the external

be used as a stabilizer and a auxiliary emusifier
