## Conclusion
The release of lupeol from nanoparticles was well described by
power law and Higuchi equation. It was not observed transport
across Caco-2 cell model at different experimental times. Best antiinflammatory effects were observed at highest dose of pure lupeol
and lowest dose of nanonutraceutical compound, this different
behavior could be related to an increased bioavailability of encapsulated lupeol in the cell interior.
