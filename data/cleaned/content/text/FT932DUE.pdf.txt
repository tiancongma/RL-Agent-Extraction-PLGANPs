 DM--Cyd

is
 more

 heightened
 as
 the

 of

present
 in

 increased.
 A
 burst

 of
 about
 60%
was
 observed
 at
 the
 highest
 concentration
 of
 the
 macrocycle.
 It
 is
probable
 that
 the
 macrocycle
 produces
 a
 non-homogeneous
 distri-
bution
 of
 the
 drug
 in
 the
 PLGA
 matrix,
 resulting
 in
 a
 more
 superﬁcial
distribution
 of
 the
 drug.
 We  should
 also
 consider
 the
 inﬂuence
exerted
 by
 the
 macrocycle
 on
 the
 morphology
 of
 the

In

 as

 through

 the
 presence
 of
 DM-
-Cyd
 produced

 in

 which
facilitated
 the
 contact
 of
 the

 of
 the

 with

release

 accelerating
 CCB

 [32]. Moreover,
a

 of

 from
 the

 (100%
 within
12
 h
 for
 all
 formulations,
 data
 not
 shown)
 could
 facilitate
 CCB
 dif-
fusion
 through
 a
 mechanism
 of
 extraction
 and
 complexation
 in
the
 aqueous
 phase.
 In

 to
 verify
 this

 the

medium
 withdrawn
 at

 was
 analyzed

 (CD)

 previous

 [26], CCB
alone
 does
 not

 in

 presence
 of
 DM--Cyd
two

 at
 240

and
 the

 positive
 at
 280
 nm.

 these

bands

 of
 the
 microenvironment
 polarity
of
 CCB
 as

 consequence
 of
 its
 inclusion
 into
 the
 DM--Cyd
 cav-
ity

 band
 was

 in

 4,

 two
 induct

were
 present
 in
 every

 prepared
 in
 the
 presence
 of
 DM-
-Cyd

 the

 of
 a

 in

After

 sustained

 was
 obtained
 at
 differ-
ent

 for
 different
 formulations.
 The

 prepared
 in
the
 absence
 of
 the
 macrocycle
 (batch
 4)
 showed
 a
 very
 slow
 rate
of
 drug
 release.
 In

 at
 the
 15th

 of
 the
 experiment,
 a
 con-
sistent
 amount
 of

 was
 still

 from

 (about

 w/w).

 be

 the

 the
 morphology
 of
 the

 In

 the

 of

 can
 produce

 high

 solubility
 of
 the

 in
 the
 PLGA

 [20]. More-
over,
 the

 of
 the

 reduces
 the
 rate

 water

 of

 The

 probably
 increased

 of

 due

 its

 to
 produce

 the

thus

8

 About

 (w/w)

(w/w)
 of

 6,
respectively.

 describes

when

[25]. However,

ﬁt

CCB
