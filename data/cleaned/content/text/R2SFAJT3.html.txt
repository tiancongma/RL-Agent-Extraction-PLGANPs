Development and evaluation of polymeric nanoparticles as a delivery system for snake envenoming prevention
Highlights
• The PLGA nanoparticles were used for the formulation of vaccine using Cerastes cerastes (Cc) venom as antigen.
• The physicochemical properties of the vaccine nanoformulation were analyzed.
• PLGA nanoparticles encapsulating Cerastes cerastes venom were used in an immunization protocol by intranasal route.
• Cc venom loaded PLGA nanoparticles induced innate and humoral responses.
• The immnoprotective effect of the vaccine formulation was evaluated against Cerastes cerastes and Vipera lebetina venoms.
