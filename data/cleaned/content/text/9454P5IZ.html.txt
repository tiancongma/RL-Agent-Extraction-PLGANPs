International Journal of Biological Macromolecules
Mucoadhesive chitosan-poly (lactic- co -glycolic acid) nanoparticles for intranasal delivery of quetiapine – Development & characterization in physiologically relevant 3D tissue models
Highlights
• Development of quetiapine hemifumarate (QF) poloxamer-chitosan poly (lactic- co -glycolic acid) nanoparticles (QF-PLGA-ISG).
• QF-PLGA-ISG revealed safe and effective uptake and permeation against RPMI-2650 cell lines and MatTek EpiNasal™ tissues.
• Intranasal (IN) QF-PLGA-NPs and QF-PLGA-ISG improved the delivery of QF in brain disorders.
• IN QF-PLGA-NPs administration could reduce QF systemic exposures and associated side effects.
