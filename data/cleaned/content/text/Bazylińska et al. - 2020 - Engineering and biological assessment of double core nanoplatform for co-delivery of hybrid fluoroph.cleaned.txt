2.2. Synthesis of TOPO stabilized up-converting NaYF4:Er3+,Yb3+NPs

TOPO stabilized NaYF4 NPs co-doped with Er3+ (2%) and Yb3+

(20%) were synthesized based on protocols described previously .
Briefly, 1 mmol of lanthanide oxides were mixed and dissolved in
~50% concentrated trifluoroacetic acid, followed by the excess water
and acid evaporation. Next, 15 ml of octadecane, 20 g of TOPO and
1.25 mmol of sodium trifluoroacetate were added into the reaction
flask. The solution was then heated to 120 degC under vacuum with
magnetic stirring for total oxygen and water removal, during which the
flask was purged with dry nitrogen every 5 min. The resulting yellow
solution
was
then
heated
to
300 degC
for
60 min.
Obtained
NaYF4:Er3+,Yb3+ NPs were collected by centrifugation (11,000 rpm),
washed several times with mixture of acetone and methanol, and finally
dispersed in chloroform.
