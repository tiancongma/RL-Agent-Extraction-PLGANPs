1.

During

 the

(e.g.

 against

(Harper,

a

 or

disease)
 (Olesen
 et

 2009).

 that

S.

that

 (MRSA)

and

 are
 becoming

 a

ﬁbronectin

 Collagen

 (CNA)
 belongs

 structurally

 to

 and

 been

 For

 CNA19
 corresponding
 to
 151–318
 collagen

 (Mw

 Da)
 and

 the
 minimum

 binding
 domain
 for

 was

 adhesin
 model
 in

 CNA19
 as

 sub-unit
 proteins
 offers
 the
 best

ﬁle
 with

 to
 the
 various
 live
 organism-based

 but
 its

 implementation
 is
 limited

 poor
 immunogenicity

 without

The
 use
 of

 have
 proven
 to

 these
 sub-unit
 vaccines
 through
 protection
 (i.e.

 in

 antigen)

 of

(Demana

Most

 are

 (10–1000

 is

 antigen-presenting

 immune

 due

 slow

 (Storni

Currently,

 adjuvant

 is

 vaccine

 for
 human

 in
 Europe
 (although

 is

 in

 US

 Synthetic
and

 can

 to

 to

 currently

O’Hagan
 et

 Moreover,

 of

 must

 with

 about
 nanotechnology
 biocompatibil-
ity
 and
 toxicity
 (Look

The
 biodegradable
 and

 polymer
 poly(dl-lactide-
co-glycolide)
 (PLGA)
 has
 been

 for
 decades
 for

 delivery
of
 a
 variety
 of
 drug

 including

 and

 (Mundargi

 It
 is

 for

 by

 and
 Drug

(FDA),

 In

PLGA

 (Perrie

Looking

 aureus-mediated

 the
