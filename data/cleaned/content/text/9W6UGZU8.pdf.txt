 for  polymeric  nanoparti-

 to explicate
 the  mechanisms
 of nanoparticle

 The

 of the  nanoparticles

 Molecular

 energy

 (MMER)

 of  alginate

 monooleate

 the  experimental

 Furthermore,

delivery

 is

the

 of

 system
