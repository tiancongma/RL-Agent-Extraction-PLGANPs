Preparation of ropivacaine loaded PLGA microspheres as controlled-release system with narrow size distribution and high loading efficiency
Highlights
• Uniform microspheres containing ropivacaine were prepared by O/W emulsion method combined with premix membrane emulsification technique.
• Solidification methods had significant influence on the inner structure of microsphere and the physical state of ropivacaine.
• For low molecular weight of PLGA , a higher EE% has been obtained because the interaction of ropivacaine-PLGA became more strengthen.
• Ropivacaine loaded microspheres with sustained drug release exhibited an excellent performance on reducing cytotoxicity.
