Colloids and Surfaces B: Biointerfaces
Amine-functionalized poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid) nanoparticles for improved cellular uptake and tumor penetration
Highlights
• Poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid)-amine (PLGA-NH 2 ) nanoparticles (NPs) were fabricated.
• Phloretin was encapsulated into the PLGA-NH 2 NPs for tumor-targeted delivery.
• PLGA-NH 2 NPs exhibited an improved cellular accumulation, compared with PLGA NPs.
• Enhanced tumor penetration efficiency in spheroids was observed in PLGA-NH 2 NPs group.
