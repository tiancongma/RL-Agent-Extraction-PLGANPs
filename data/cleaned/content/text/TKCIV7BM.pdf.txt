1.
 Introduction
The

 of

 commonly

 sys-

 and
 fast

 However,

 More

 In

are

 However,

 in

cles

 poly(d,l-lactide-co-glycolide
 acid)
 (PLGA)
 (Anderson

 et

 The

 this

 can
 be

 its

 and

dation

 Indeed,
 humans
 can
 easily

 (lactic

 and

 The

Depending
